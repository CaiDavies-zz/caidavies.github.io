About me

I’m a startup loving designer, who enjoys designing great products. Currently I help solve problems at Hassle.com, the easiest way for you to book a cleaner online.

Before Hassle.com, I worked at Absolute Radio, helping to design and develop a great experience in listening to online radio. I have designed for screens, physical products and installations, including most recently an exhibition in the London Design Museum.

I’m a strong advocator of measuring what you ship; setting metrics allows the objectivity in design; If you’d like to hear more, feel free to contact me.

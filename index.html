About me

I’m a startup loving designer, who enjoys designing great products. I most recently helped solve problems at carwow.co.uk, the easiest way for you to buy a new car online.

Previously I worked at Hassle.com & Absolute Radio. I've designed for screens, physical products and installations – including an exhibition in the London Design Museum.

I’m a strong advocator of measuring what you ship; setting metrics allows the objectivity in design; If you'd like to hear more, feel free to contact me.
